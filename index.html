Open Roles

Roles we need help with right now

General Distro Volunteer
Set up tables, organize inventory, and help neighbours find what they need
Go in and out of the bus to restock items throughout the distro
Stay for take-down and do a quick inventory count at the end
Available most Saturdays at Grandview Park, Oppenheimer Park, or near Trillium Park — shifts run about 3 hours
Supply Runner
Do regular supply runs to stores around Vancouver (reimbursement provided)
Pick up donated items from our drop-off spots — Cross & Crows, Wildfires Bookshop, and the private residence on 12th Ave East
Requires regular access to a vehicle and availability at least 2x per month
Fundraising Team
Brainstorm and execute fundraising ideas — past initiatives include talent shows, merch sales, online raffles, and social media callouts
Work mostly remotely through Discord and occasional online meetings
Ramp up hours when an event or initiative is approaching — otherwise 1–3 hours per week
Food Partner
Prep and serve food at one distro per month — either Trillium Park (2nd Saturday) or Oppenheimer Park (4th Saturday)
Food can be self-funded or you can run a callout for dedicated food funds — current partners spend around $100/month
Examples: soup, pizza, sandwiches, fruit — whatever you can make work
Volunteer Coordinator
Check the volunteer schedule regularly and send callouts via email or Discord to fill open shifts
Plan quarterly volunteer meetings and coordinate new volunteer onboarding as needed
Work is mostly remote and async — 1 to 3 hours per week, more when organizing events
Only apply if you can commit consistently — the team depends on this role staying active
