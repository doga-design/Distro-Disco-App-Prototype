DD's Standards

Condition

Clean, dry, and functional. No mold, stains, tears, pests, or heavy wear. Clothing must be weather-appropriate. Hygiene, menstrual, and first-aid items must be new and unopened.

Packaging

Bag or box your donations. Seal and label if you can (e.g. “Clothing – M/L”). Keep categories separate.

We can’t take

Used underwear, damaged footwear, expired food or medicine, electronics, furniture, appliances, liquid chemicals, or anything broken. Not sure? Reach out before you drop off.

Only donate what you’d be happy to receive yourself.

Volunteer Oath

  • I understand that volunteering with Distro Disco may involve physical activity including lifting, carrying, and standing for extended periods.
  • I understand that my commitment requires responding to callouts in a timely manner and showing up to events I have confirmed.
  • I understand that DD will contact me via phone for scheduling, updates, and event information.
  • I understand that donations I handle must be treated with care and distributed with respect for the people receiving them.
  • I understand that I represent Distro Disco's values in the community and will conduct myself accordingly.
